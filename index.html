Eco Goals & Challenges

Join climate missions to speed up your environmental recovery. Challenge yourself to live green.

🚫🛍️ 7 Days

7-Day Plastic Free Challenge

Commit to zero single-use plastics, including straws, grocery carrier bags, and plastic bottles, for 7 consecutive days.

🚍🚂 1 Week

Public Transport Week

Swap out your private car for trains, buses, carpools, walking, or cycling for all commutes during the work week.

🔌💡 Flexible

Save Electricity Challenge

Unplug phantom electronics, use energy-saver appliances, wash clothes in cold water, and transition to daylight.

🥦🍲 5 Days

Plant-Based Meal Challenge

Reduce agricultural greenhouse emissions by eating entirely plant-based recipes for breakfasts, lunches, and dinners.

📦♻️ Ongoing

Recycle More Challenge

Separate paper, cardboard, metal tins, and glass. Upcycle packages and reduce household landfill waste by half.
